The Concept

A flower that sees — and speaks.

Hidden inside the petals of a beautiful decorative flower is a precision PIR motion sensor and a powerful audio speaker. The intruder sees décor. Your home sees danger — and responds instantly.

1
BloomGuard Sits Beautifully in Your Space
Placed on a shelf, windowsill, garden step, or entrance — it looks like an ordinary decorative flower. Visitors and family see décor. The sensor watches silently, always on.
2
Motion Sensor Detects an Intruder
The hidden PIR (Passive Infrared) sensor inside the flower head detects human body heat and movement within its detection zone — up to 8 metres. Within less than one second, it knows someone is there.
3
A Powerful Voice Warning Plays Immediately
A loud, authoritative recorded voice erupts from the flower — warning the intruder that they have been detected, they are being recorded, and the owner has been alerted. Most intruders flee immediately.
4
Escalates if They Don't Leave
If motion continues, a second and third warning plays — each more urgent. The third warning triggers the full alarm siren alongside the voice, creating maximum deterrence.
