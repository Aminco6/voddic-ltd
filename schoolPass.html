Step-by-Step Flow

Secure entry & exit in under 10 seconds.

The entire process — from parent arriving at the gate to the classroom announcement — takes less time than writing a name in a paper book.

🏫
Parent / Guardian Arrives at Gate
Security or the gate attendant opens the SchoolPass tablet app. No internet connection needed — it runs entirely offline on the Android device.
Offline APK · No WiFi
🔍
Security Searches the Student Name
The gate attendant types the student's name or scrolls through the list. Each student's photo, class, and approved guardian list appears instantly from the local database.
Local Database · Instant Search
🔐
Parent or Guardian Enters Their Secret PIN
The parent types their personal PIN — set during registration. Only registered guardians with the correct PIN can sign a student in or out. No PIN match, no access. Wrong PIN attempts are logged.
PIN Secured · Per Guardian
✅
Student Logged IN — Entry Timestamped
The system logs the exact date and time of entry, the guardian's name, and the PIN verified. A full digital audit trail is built automatically with zero manual effort from staff.
Digital Log · Auto Timestamp
📢
ESP32 Hub Announces Arrival School-Wide
The gate terminal sends a signal to the ESP32 announcement hub. Speakers throughout the school broadcast the student's name and class — confirming safe arrival to teachers and staff automatically.
ESP32 Hub · PA Announcement
🚪
Dismissal: Classroom Announcement for Pickup
At closing, the same process runs in reverse. After PIN verification at the gate, the ESP32 hub in the student's specific classroom announces their name to come to the gate — no manual teacher calling needed.
Classroom Speaker · Smart Dismissal

Core Features

Everything your school gate needs. Nothing more.

Built lean, built for schools that need reliability, security, and simplicity — not complexity.

📵
100% Offline — No Internet Ever Needed
The Android APK runs entirely from the device's local storage. No WiFi, no data, no cloud dependency. It works on the cheapest Android tablet you have in the school.
🔐
PIN-Secured Per Guardian
Every registered parent or guardian gets a unique 4-digit PIN. Only the PIN holder can sign their child in or out. Unverified adults cannot remove a student from school premises.
📢
ESP32 PA Announcement Hub
The gate tablet communicates with the ESP32 hub via Bluetooth or local RF — no internet required. Speakers at the entrance and in classrooms announce arrivals and dismissals automatically.
🏫
Classroom-Specific Dismissal Calls
When a parent checks out a student at closing, the ESP32 unit in that student's specific classroom announces their name — so only the right child is called, no disruption to other classes.
📋
Automatic Digital Attendance Log
Every entry and exit is timestamped, tied to a guardian name, and stored locally. Exportable daily, weekly, or termly reports. No more lost paper registers or illegible handwriting.
⚠️
Wrong PIN Alert & Lockout
Three consecutive wrong PIN attempts triggers an alert and temporarily locks the profile. Security is notified immediately — preventing brute-force or impersonation attempts at the gate.

Offline-First Architecture

No internet. No problem. Ever.

We built SchoolPass from the ground up for schools where internet is unreliable, expensive, or simply unavailable. The system never needs a connection to function.

📱
Android APK — Installs Once, Works Forever
Install the APK on any Android tablet or phone at the gate. All student data, guardian PINs, and logs are stored locally. No app store, no updates required unless you want new features.
📡
ESP32 Hub Connects via Bluetooth / RF
The gate tablet communicates with the ESP32 announcement hub using Bluetooth or a local RF signal — completely bypassing the internet. If one hub is in the corridor and one is in each classroom, they all receive the signal.
🔋
Works Through Power Interruptions
The Android device runs on battery. The ESP32 hub is backed by a small rechargeable cell. NEPA outages don't stop the school gate from operating securely.
💾
Data Export When Needed
When you want reports, connect to a local hotspot and export the term's attendance data as a spreadsheet or PDF. No cloud needed — you control your data entirely.
