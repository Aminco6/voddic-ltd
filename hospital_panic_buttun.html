System Operation

Patient presses. Nurse knows. In seconds.

The entire sequence — from button press to nurse at the bedside — is automated, room-specific, and requires no action from anyone except the patient pressing the button.

1
Patient Presses the Bedside Call Button
A compact wireless button is placed within arm's reach of every patient. One press — no password, no phone, no confusion — even for elderly or incapacitated patients.
2
Instant Wireless Alert to the Nurse Station
The signal transmits wirelessly and immediately to the central control unit at the nurse station, identifying the exact ward, room, and bed number.
3
Voice Announcement Plays Through Speakers
A clear audio announcement broadcasts the patient's location to nursing staff — eliminating guesswork and ensuring every nurse within earshot knows exactly where to go.
4
Nurse Responds to the Right Bed, Immediately
No phone calls, no running down the wrong corridor, no delays. Your staff responds faster, more confidently, and with far less anxiety for the patient waiting.

Ward Applications

Every ward. Every patient. Every call answered.

From maternity to intensive care, the Voddic Patient Call System adapts to the unique demands of every clinical environment in your hospital.

🧪
General & Medical Wards
Patients recovering from surgery or illness can call for assistance — pain medication, repositioning, bedpan — without straining or shouting.
❤️
ICU & High Dependency
Even minimally conscious or restrained patients can summon attention. Every alert is audible instantly at the nurse station regardless of ward size.
🍼
Maternity & Obstetrics
Mothers in labour or post-delivery can request assistance immediately. Midwives and nurses respond faster, improving outcomes and patient confidence.

Paediatric Ward
Buttons designed to be simple enough for parents or older children to operate. Keeps anxious parents connected to nursing staff at all times.

Orthopaedic & Rehabilitation

Private & VIP Rooms
Premium patient experience with discreet, immediate response. A must-have for hospitals looking to differentiate their private ward service level.
