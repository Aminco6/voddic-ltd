▸ THE DEVICE

Intelligent. Vocal. Relentless.

VoxGuard detects intruders and responds with calibrated voice warnings before triggering the alarm — giving you control over how your security escalates. Every unit is custom-configured to your exact specifications before delivery.

  • PIR motion detection with adjustable sensitivity zone
  • Custom voice warning messages (your language, your tone)
  • Configurable 1–3 stage warning sequence before full alarm
  • Multiple alarm siren types to choose from
  • Indoor & outdoor installation options
  • Low power consumption, mains or battery operable
  • Built and tested locally — delivered directly to you

▸ COMMON QUESTIONS

FAQ

What is the commitment fee? ▶
The commitment fee is 50% of the total price (₦15,000) paid upfront to reserve your build slot and cover the cost of custom configuration. The remaining ₦15,000 is only paid when your unit is delivered and you're satisfied with it.
Can I get a refund if I change my mind? ▶
Because each unit is custom-built to your specification, the commitment fee is non-refundable once production begins (usually 48 hours after order confirmation). However, if we cannot fulfil your order, you will receive a full refund.
How long does delivery take? ▶
Custom builds typically take 5–14 working days from order confirmation depending on your specifications and location. You'll receive updates at every stage.
Can I change my audio warning or alarm type after ordering? ▶
Yes — within 48 hours of order confirmation. After production begins, changes may require additional time but we'll always work with you.
How is the device installed? ▶
VoxGuard is designed for easy self-installation with mounting hardware included. For complex setups, we can arrange installation support locally. Detailed instructions are included with every unit.
What areas do you deliver to? ▶
We currently deliver across Nigeria. Delivery fees vary by location and will be communicated at the time of order confirmation. Interstate deliveries are handled through reliable courier services.
