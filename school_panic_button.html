System Operation

One press. Staff responds. Student protected.

The entire sequence is instant, location-specific, initiator-specific, and requires nothing from a student in distress except pressing a single button — even silently.

1
Student Presses the Emergency Button
A compact SOS button is installed in classrooms, hostels, toilets, corridors, and other campus zones. One press — no PIN, no phone, even usable under duress or in fear.
2
Instant Alert to Security & Admin Station
The signal transmits wirelessly and immediately to the security control unit, identifying the exact campus zone, block, and location where the button was pressed.
3
Voice Announcement or Silent Alert Activates
Depending on configuration, either a public PA announcement or a silent staff-only alert notifies the right people — security, a teacher, the admin — immediately and precisely.
4
Staff Dispatched to the Exact Location
No guessing, no confusion. Security and staff respond to the exact zone within seconds — stopping abuse, harassment, fights, or any threat before it escalates further.
▸ Live Announcement Demo

Press play to hear a real Voddic campus safety announcement — broadcast to security and staff the instant a student presses the emergency button.
"Attention please, attention please. A student at Toilet Block B has pressed the emergency button. Staff attention might be needed. Please respond immediately. Thank you."
A silent mode is also available — alerts go only to staff devices and the security station without a public announcement, protecting student privacy and preventing escalation.
Incidents Covered

Every threat. One button. Immediate response.

From physical violence to silent psychological abuse — the Voddic system empowers students and staff to trigger help for any campus safety incident.

👊
HIGH RISK
Physical Assault & Fighting
Students involved in or witnessing a physical fight or violent assault can trigger an immediate staff response before serious injury occurs.
😰
HIGH RISK
Sexual Harassment & Abuse
A discreet, silent alert option allows victims of sexual harassment or abuse to call for help without drawing attention or risking retaliation from an abuser.
😤
MEDIUM RISK
Bullying & Intimidation
Students being bullied, threatened, or coerced can trigger an alert from a classroom, toilet, or corridor — stopping the intimidation before it worsens.
🔪
HIGH RISK
Weapon or Armed Threat
If a student or staff member detects a weapon or armed threat on campus, a single silent press brings security without alerting the threat.
🆘
HIGH RISK
Unauthorised Intruder
Anyone spotting an unknown or unauthorised person on campus can silently alert security before the individual moves further into the school.
💔
MEDIUM RISK
Mental Health Crisis
A student showing signs of self-harm, severe distress, or a mental health breakdown can be referred to pastoral care staff quietly and immediately.
Campus Coverage

Protection in every corner of your campus.

SOS buttons are installed across all high-risk campus zones — not just classrooms. Because danger doesn't only happen in class.

🏫
Classrooms
Teacher or student can trigger alert during intimidation, threats, or disruptive incidents mid-lesson.
🚻
Toilets & Bathrooms
The highest-risk blind spot on any campus. Discreet buttons provide coverage where staff cannot be present.
🏠
Hostels & Dormitories
Nighttime incidents, room-based abuse, or dormitory fights are reported instantly without leaving the room.
🛤️
Corridors & Stairwells
Common zones for confrontation, bullying, and unsupervised harassment between classes or after hours.
⚽
Sports Fields
Remote from main buildings — fights or accidents during sports activities can be reported immediately.
📚
Library & Study Areas
Quiet zones where intimidation or coercion often goes unnoticed. A silent SOS reaches security without disruption.
🍽️
Canteen & Dining Hall
Large gathering areas where confrontations and bullying frequently occur during unsupervised break periods.
🚪
School Gates & Perimeter
Entry points and boundaries — alerts for unauthorised access, off-campus intimidation, or suspicious activity near school grounds.
