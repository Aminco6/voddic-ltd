System Operation

From button press to on-site response — in seconds.

Every step is automated, room-specific, and crystal clear. Your staff always knows exactly where help is needed.

1
Guest or Staff Presses Panic Button
A compact, discreet wireless button is installed in each room. One press is all it takes — no phone needed, no PIN, no delay.
2
Instant Wireless Signal to Reception
The alert transmits instantly to the central reception control unit, which identifies the exact room number without ambiguity.
3
Voice Announcement Plays Automatically
Speakers broadcast a clear, calm voice message — naming the exact room — so every nearby staff member hears and understands immediately.
4
Staff Dispatched Within Seconds
Security or front desk personnel respond without confusion — exact location known, no radio chatter needed, no guesswork.
▸ Live Announcement Demo
Hear exactly what your reception team hears.
Press play to hear a real-world Voddic system announcement — the same message your speakers would broadcast the moment a panic button is pressed.
"Attention please, attention please. A panic button has been pressed in Room 203. Security attention might be needed. Thank you."
The announcement system uses high-clarity audio output, automatically triggered by the central unit. Room numbers, floor levels, and custom messages can be configured to match your hotel's operational language.

Use Cases

One system. Every emergency scenario.

Voddic SOS button handles the full spectrum of hospitality safety incidents — discretely, instantly, and without relying on guests to know what to do.

🆘
Guest Distress
A guest in fear or danger presses the in-room button for immediate security response — no phone call needed.
🏥
Medical Emergency
A single press alerts staff to a health emergency in the exact room, enabling first-aid response within seconds.
🤫
Staff Duress
Housekeeping and room service staff carry wearable buttons for discreet help signals in uncomfortable or dangerous situations.
🔒
Security Breach
Unauthorized access or suspicious activity can trigger the alert, notifying security to lock down the affected area immediately.
🔇
Silent Distress Signal
For sensitive situations where making noise could escalate danger — a silent alert option notifies only the control room.
🧹
Housekeeping Coordination
Alerts can also be used for room-ready notifications, coordinating service staff efficiently across floors.
